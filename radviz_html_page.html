What is a RadViz?

RadViz is a non-linear visualization tool that displays data on three or more attributes in a two-dimensional projection. While the attributes are represented through equally-spaced points along the perimeter of a circle, the actual data instances are positioned within the circle according to their proportional composition of each attribute. RadViz is a powerful tool that is highly effective at revealing clusters and outliers in a highly-dimensional dataset.
